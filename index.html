About Us

The global challenges of climate change, resource depletion, and environmental degradation require urgent, innovative solutions. This conference aims to bring together thought leaders, industry professionals, policymakers, and researchers to discuss and propose actionable strategies for a sustainable future. Focus will be on the intersection of sustainability, environment, and energy, exploring how technological advancements, policy frameworks, and collaborative efforts can drive meaningful change. The conference will explore cutting-edge research, best practices, and the latest developments in sustainable energy technologies, environmental protection, and resource conservation.

Conference Submissions

1st International Conference on Sustainability, Environment, and Energy (ICASE 2025) is a multidisciplinary forum addressing global challenges such as climate change, resource depletion, and environmental degradation. The conference will bring together researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss and develop innovative strategies for a sustainable future. ICASE 2025 will focus on: Sustainable Materials and Manufacturing – Advancing eco-friendly materials and sustainable production processes. Renewable and Clean Energy Technologies – Exploring innovations in solar, wind, hydrogen, and other clean energy solutions. Nanotechnology and Advanced Technologies for Sustainability – Leveraging cutting-edge advancements for environmental and energy applications. Climate Change Mitigation and Carbon Capture – Addressing emissions reduction and climate resilience strategies. Environmental Monitoring and Data Analytics – Utilizing big data, AI, and IoT for effective environmental management. Through expert-led sessions, peer-reviewed research, and collaborative discussions, ICASE 2025 will serve as a platform for knowledge exchange and impactful solutions for a more sustainable world.
